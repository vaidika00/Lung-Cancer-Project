Lung Cancer Detector and Drug Advisor(LCDA)

The Lung Cancer Detection and Generic Drug Recommendation System (LDRS) is a machine learning based system that uses medical images and patient data to detect lung cancer and recommend generic drugs for its treatment. The LDRS system is still under development, but it has the potential to improve the early detection and treatment of lung cancer.
